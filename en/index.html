TPP 2026

Theorem Proving and Provers Meeting

A meeting for researchers and users of theorem proving and proof assistants.

Event Information

  • Date: Monday, November 16, 2026 to Tuesday, November 17, 2026
  • Co-host: RIKEN Center for Advanced Intelligence Project (Riken AIP)
  • Venue: RIKEN Center for Advanced Intelligence Project (Riken AIP), Tokyo Office
  • Address: 15th Floor, Mitsubishi Building, 1-4-1 Nihonbashi 1-chome, Chuo-ku, Tokyo 103-0027, Japan (Google Maps)
  • TPP-mark: Details to be announced

Overview

TPP has been held annually since 2005 as a place for people involved in theorem proving: researchers, implementers, and users. We focus on rigorous, computational, and practical discussions.
